## Prompt 01 — Boho Sunset Circles
*[Style reference: v-art-001]*

**Prompt:**
Minimalist abstract print with six circular and semi-circular elements arranged in two vertical columns of three on white background. Top row: solid sage green circle left, smaller terracotta circle right. Middle row: off-white cream circle left, terracotta semi-circle flat-side-down right. Bottom row: terracotta solid circle left, sage green textured circle with horizontal line detail right. Earthy muted boho-modern palette. Clean graphic design composition.

## Bonus: Composition Variation Guide

**For each prompt above, try these variations:**
- Swap the dominant color for its complement
- Change aspect ratio from 3:4 to 4:3 for wider compositions
- Remove one element to increase negative space
- Add a thin black or gold line as a divider element
